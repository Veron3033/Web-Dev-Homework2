Story

In the aftermath of a personal tragedy, David Martinez chooses to take control of his own life by becoming an edgerunner and entering the criminal underworld of Night City. Cyberpunk: Edgerunners asks just how far you can go to overcome your physical and emotional limits, and what the pursuit of fame within a city ruled by greed and disrespect for human life can lead to.

Creators

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners is an award-winning Netflix anime created in collaboration between CD PROJEKT RED and the legendary Japanese animation studio TRIGGER. Known for critically acclaimed TV series, including Little Witch Academia and Delicious in Dungeon, as well as the movies Promare and Gridman Universe, brought Night City to life thanks to an exquisite creator team and cast.
