Story

CIn the aftermath of a personal tragedy, David Martinez chooses to take control of his own life by becoming an edgerunner and entering the criminal underworld of Night City. Cyberpunk: Edgerunners asks just how far you can go to overcome your physical and emotional limits, and what the pursuit of fame within a city ruled by greed and disrespect for human life can lead to.

Creators

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners is an award-winning Netflix anime created in collaboration between CD PROJEKT RED and the legendary Japanese animation studio TRIGGER.
